About us

Nuverde LLC. was established in July 2015 in Austin, Texas with the intent of becoming partners with companies and scientists with new and disruptive technology products.

We provide assistance to companies/inventors finding angel investor funding, business partners or customers for their new products.

NuVerde LLC. is a Disabled Veteran company that envisions creating opportunities for other veterans to sell these products or establish their own turnkey business.
